🦅 OUR MISSION

The truth deserves a wider audience.

What Ravenmails is, and the promise behind it.

Every day, important science and big questions get buried in journals — or drowned out by noise and misinformation. Most people never get the clear version. Ravenmails exists to fix that.

We cover science, the issues that matter, and the facts behind viral claims — explained in plain language, checked against the evidence, and told from a Filipino point of view with an eye on the world. No jargon walls, no paywalls. Just clear, honest writing, free for everyone.

What we stand for

🆓

Free

No paywall, ever. Good information should not depend on who can pay.

🛡️

Reliable

Verified against primary sources and at least two independent ones before we publish.

🔍

Transparent

We show our sources, disclose our methods, and are open about AI use.

↩️

Accountable

We correct mistakes fast and in the open, and invite you to call them out.

Why this matters

Good information is hard to find and easy to fake. Real research stays locked behind jargon and paywalls, while misinformation spreads fast and free. Ravenmails stands in that gap — making the real story clear, citing where it comes from, and always crediting the people who did the work. We want to be the most reliable place a Filipino can turn to make sense of science and the big issues.

Who's behind Ravenmails

Ravenmails is produced and edited by the Ravenmails team. We publish under the team's name, and the team takes full editorial responsibility for everything here. Our work is guided by a published set of editorial standards.

How we're funded

Ravenmails is free to read and supported by clearly-labeled, non-intrusive advertising — kept strictly separate from our journalism. Advertisers get no say over coverage, we don't run sponsored content disguised as news, and we never take money in exchange for a story or a verdict. Ads keep Ravenmails free and independent, so it can sustain itself without a paywall or a patron. See Independence & funding for the full policy.
