Corrections & clarifications

When we get something wrong, we fix it fast — and record it here, in the open.

Accountability isn't a footnote at Ravenmails — it's part of the promise. Significant corrections and clarifications are logged on this page so anyone can see what changed and when.

How corrections work

  • We update the article itself and note what was changed.
  • Significant fixes — anything affecting facts, figures, names, or a verdict — are logged here with the date.
  • Minor typo fixes that don't change meaning are made quietly and not logged.
  • If new evidence changes a fact-check verdict, we update the verdict and explain why.
